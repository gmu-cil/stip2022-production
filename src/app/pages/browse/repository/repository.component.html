Imprint

1. "1957 Victim Names Dictionary" since 2000 began to collect, edit, due to the continuous evolution of editing ideas, tailoring methods, but also due to the lack of time and resources, so more than ten years of style, format, arrangement, inevitably there are inconsistent situations, errors, omissions, and even irregularities, these readers are more critical and corrected, and gradually corrected and overcome.

2. The project has received the care and guidance of experts and scholars such as Professor Ding Shu; the help of teachers of the China Research Service Center of the University of Chinese of Hong Kong and colleagues of the Wuqi Society; and the support of the victims and their descendants in providing historical materials and memoirs in 1957.

3. Professor Song Yongyi's "Database of China's Rightist Movement 1957-" , the "Internal Reference" collected by the China Research Service Center of the University of Chinese of Hong Kong, the relevant newspaper books, the "Traces of the Past" edited and published by Tieliu and the Old Man of Beijing May 7, and Professor Guo Dehong's "Chronicle of the "Anti-Rightist Movement" (book manuscript), etc., are of great help to this project. The China Research Service Center also provides technical support.

4. In 2019, the 1957 Great Dictionary of Victim Names was funded by the American Labor Reform Research Foundation to publish a database version. I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to the Laogai Research Foundation and colleagues, all relevant scholars, teachers, friends and volunteers! And please continue to help, support!

版本说明

1.《1957年受難者姓名大辭典》自2000年开始收集、编辑，由于编辑思路、剪裁方法的不断演进，也由于时间、资源之不足，所以十多年间的体例、格式、编排，难免有前后不统一的情况，错误、遗漏、甚至不规范，这些请读者多多批评指正，逐渐纠正、克服。

2.本项目得到丁抒教授等专家学者的关心、指导；得到香港中文大学中国研究服务中心老师、五七学社同仁的帮助；得到1957年受难者及其后人在提供史料、回忆录等方面的支持。

3.宋永毅教授主编之《中国反右运动数据库1957-》、香港中文大学中国研究服务中心收藏的《内部参考》及有关报刊书籍、铁流和北京五七老人编辑出版的《往事微痕》、郭德宏教授《“反右运动”编年史》（书稿）等，对本项目帮助尤大。中国研究服务中心还提供有关技术支援。

4.2019年《1957年受難者姓名大辭典》得到美国劳改研究基金会资助，出版数据库版。向劳改研究基金会及同仁，各有关学者、老师、朋友、义工表示衷心感谢！并请继续帮助、支持！
